About NeuroScan AI

Revolutionizing brain tumor detection through artificial intelligence

Our Mission

NeuroScan AI is a cutting-edge deep learning project designed to assist in the early detection and classification of brain tumors from MRI scans. Our tool uses advanced neural network architectures to analyze medical images and identify potential tumors with high accuracy.

This project represents the intersection of artificial intelligence and healthcare, demonstrating how machine learning can support medical professionals in making faster, more accurate diagnoses.

Important: This is a research and educational tool. Always seek professional medical advice.

Dataset Information

Our model is trained on a curated dataset of 7,200 brain MRI images from multiple trusted sources:

  • Figshare Brain MRI Dataset - Public research dataset
  • SARTAJ Dataset - Brain tumor classification MRI
  • Br35H Dataset - Brain tumor detection collection
